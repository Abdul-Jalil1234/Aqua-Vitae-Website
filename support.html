    Support Us

    Every contribution — a kit, a borehole, an hour of your time — moves a family closer to safe drinking water. Here's how to join the mission.

    Six Ways In

    Ways to Support

    Choose the contribution that fits you best — funding, infrastructure, expertise, or advocacy all move the mission forward.

    Donate Treatment Kits

    Directly fund the purification kits that give families immediate, life-saving protection from contaminated water.

    Sponsor a Borehole

    Contribute toward mechanized boreholes that deliver long-term, sustainable water access to an entire village.

    Fund a Workshop

    Cover the cost of a full health-education session — facilitator training, materials, and local transport.

    Volunteer Your Expertise

    Public health professionals, engineers, and educators are welcome to contribute time directly to our programs.

    Partner With Us

    NGOs, corporations and institutions can explore formal partnerships to co-fund infrastructure or amplify advocacy.

    Spread the Word

    Awareness is a resource too — sharing our mission helps us reach new donors, partners, and volunteers.

    Before You Reach Out

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I donate water treatment kits?
    Use the Contact page and select “Donating treatment kits” as your topic — our team will send secure payment details and a confirmation once your kits are dispatched.
    What does sponsoring a borehole actually fund?
    Your sponsorship goes toward drilling, mechanized pump installation, and a 12-month maintenance plan carried out with our partner NGOs and local engineers.
    Can I volunteer without a technical background?
    Yes — we welcome help with workshop facilitation, translation, logistics, and community outreach, in addition to public health and engineering expertise.
    Do you provide receipts for donations?
    Yes, every contribution receives a confirmation and receipt by email, itemising exactly what your donation funded.
    Which regions are you currently operating in?
    See the “Current Work” section on our Mission page for our active districts and planned expansion regions over the next 18–24 months.
