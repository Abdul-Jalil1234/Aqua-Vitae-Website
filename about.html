    About Us

    The People Behind the Mission

    A team of software engineering students who believe a well-built website can move a real-world mission forward — one line of code, one workshop, one family at a time.

    Founder & Project Lead

    Abdul-Jalil Fuseini

    My journey into this work started long before I ever wrote a line of code. Growing up near communities where a simple stomach bug could mean a week out of school, I saw firsthand how something as basic as water access shapes every other outcome in a person's life — health, education, opportunity.

    Studying Software Engineering gave me a new lens on an old problem: technology can't dig a borehole, but it can organize the people, funding, and knowledge that do. Aqua Vitae Initiative is where that belief meets a real project — a mission-driven website built by students, for a cause we actually care about.

    CO FOUNDER

    Noelle Ingabire

    Noelle is a Data Analyst and Researcher dedicated to advancing global health equity by tackling the water crisis across the African continent. Deeply moved by recent global reports showing that over 400 million people in Africa still lack access to basic safe drinking water, she is committed to leveraging data to transform day-to-day lives and public health outcomes.

    Noelle believes that in the digital era, rigorous research can pinpoint whether a community's crisis stems from absolute water scarcity or systemic distribution failures. By gathering, analyzing, and maintaining robust datasets, she equips teams to influence government strategies, optimize infrastructure budgets, and build unassailable cases that inspire investors, mobilize volunteers, and unlock vital funding.
